Red Spots

Red Spots (Cherry Angioma, “Red Mole”) on the skin may be due to several causes. One type which is fairly common, especially on the chest, abdomen, and sometimes the face is the cherry angioma. These red spots are frequently multiple and often run in families. A “Red Mole” are the result of an overgrowth of capillaries, a type of blood vessel.

Laser removal of these red skin spots is easily performed, and laser treatment is rapid.

At Svetlana’s Skin Secrets we use AFT/IPL intensive pulsed light (IPL) or advanced fluroscent technology (AFT).
